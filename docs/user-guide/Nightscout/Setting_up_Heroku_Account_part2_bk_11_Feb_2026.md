## **Now we are going to create a Heroku account.**


###1. First you will need go to Heroku web page and create an account for yourself  <a href=" https://signup.heroku.com" target="_blank" title="Heroku Account">Click Here </a> <span style="background-color:#26AF06">**And enter all your account details**</span> .<br><Br>


<a href="https://signup.heroku.com/" target="_blank">
  <img width="600" height="Auto" border="0" align="center"  src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/df0967ae-dee2-4cfb-aebe-f3553946c90c" title="Sign up a Heroku Account"/></a>


Enter your:<br>
First Name, last name, email address
“Role” and “Primary development language” can be anything, but “Hobbyist” and “Node js” are the most appropriate choices. <span style="background-color:#26AF06">**And click on Create your free Account**</span><br>

###2. Now go to verify your account via the link in the verification email that’s sent to you, and then set your password.<br>

<img width="500" height="Auto" border="0" align="center"  src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f9f37d11-d433-4d5a-ae9e-ffddc0041511" title="Set Password Up"/></a><br>


###3. Click <span style="background-color:#26AF06">**Later**</span> for Secure your Account this can be done later!<br>

<img width="500" height="Auto" border="0" align="center"  src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/da90b4ab-f8ab-44de-8a5c-cd87787187c5" title="Secure your Account Later"/></a><br>


###4. Your Heroku page will open, now click on your profile picture, in the top right corner, and <span style="background-color:#26AF06">**Select Account Settings**</span><br><br>

<img width="600" height="Auto" border="0" align="center"  src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/33381630-7e67-4760-ad38-97df63313377" title="Select Account Settings"/></a><br><br>


??? info "Notes"

    Unless you willingly select billable features, your credit card information is only used for verifications and you will not be charged anything. Worst case you’ll be charged £0.00 This may of changed now in 2025, has Heroku started to charge for this service, so please check!  <br>


<br>

###5.  Click on <span style="background-color:#26AF06">**Billing**</span><br>

<img width="400" height="Auto" border="0" align="center"  src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f26d5ebb-79bb-4f54-ae5e-c21bc557ada5" title="Select Billing"/></a><br>


###6.  Click on your <span style="background-color:#26AF06">**Credit Card Details**</span><br>

<img width="600" height="Auto" border="0" align="center"  src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/63fc9911-3183-4876-b2c3-f6a2dadd3848" title="Add your Credit Card Details"/></a><br>

###7.  Enter your <span style="background-color:#26AF06">**Credit Card Details**</span> and click Save Details<br>

<img width="400" height="Auto" border="0" align="center"  src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1b462931-bdea-4485-b04d-d27b865c71bb" title="Enter Card Details"/></a><br>
