    Setting Up Tidepool

    Intro

    Key Offerings:

    1. Tidepool Platform – A free, cloud-based application that aggregates diabetes device data (from insulin pumps, CGMs, and glucose meters) into one easy-to-use dashboard.

    2. Tidepool Loop – An open-source automated insulin delivery (AID) system that helps people with diabetes manage insulin dosing more effectively.

    3. Big Data Donation Project – A program where users can anonymously donate their diabetes data to accelerate diabetes research.



    First Step:

    You will need to choose first between what you want to use with Tidepool to connect to it. There are several ways, but I will only be explaining two methods.

    Nightscout and Dexcom Clarity are the two main ones we need to setup first before we can connect to Tidepool.

    Step 1: Download the Dexcom Clarity App

    For Smartphones:
    iOS (iPhone): Available on the App Store.
    Android: Available on Google Play.
    For Computers: Access via the Dexcom Clarity Website.

    Step 2: Create or Log in to Your Dexcom Account

    If you’re new to Nightscout, then visit Nightscout Docs Website
    If you’re new to Dexcom, sign up for an account at Dexcom Clarity Website.
    If you already have a Dexcom account (used for the Dexcom G6/G7 app), log in with the same credentials.

    Step 3: Connect Your Dexcom CGM

    If using the Dexcom G6/G7 app:
    Clarity automatically syncs with your Dexcom CGM data if you’re logged into the same account.
    If using a receiver or pump:
    Manually upload data via the Clarity website (requires a USB connection). And for you to download their dexcom-uploader-4.6.0 software from Clarity Web site.



    Step 4: Share Data (Optional)

    You can invite doctors, caregivers, or family members to view your reports:
    Go to "Share" in the Clarity app or website.

    Enter their email address.
    Choose their access level (view-only or full access)

    Step 5: View Reports & Insights

    Dexcom Clarity provides:

    ✔ Daily glucose trends
    ✔ Time in Range (TIR) reports
    ✔ Hypo/Hyperglycemia analysis
    ✔ Average glucose & GMI (Glucose Management Indicator)
    Reports update every 3 hours when connected to your CGM


    Share Dexcom Data to Tidepool

    To share Dexcom data with Tidepool, you need to connect your Dexcom Clarity account to your Tidepool account.
    This is done through the Tidepool Web interface and requires you to log into your Dexcom Clarity account within the Tidepool platform. Once connected, Tidepool will automatically fetch your Dexcom data.

    Here's a step-by-step guide:

    1. Open Tidepool Web: Go to Visit tidepool.org

    2. in a web browser (Chrome or Edge) and log in to your Tidepool account.

    3. Connect a Device > Account: Navigate to the “Devices” "Connect a Device Account" section.

    4. Connect Dexcom: Click on the "Connect" button next to the Dexcom logo.


    1. Dexcom Login: You'll be redirected to a Dexcom login page. Enter your Dexcom Clarity account credentials (username and password).

    2. On my own setup I used Nightscout to connect to Tidepool and not Dexcoms, but it is done in the same manner


    Getting Started

    If you have a Tidepool account, you can automatically upload your data and share it with your diabetologist ,endocrinologist.
    If xDrip+ can display basal information from an external source such as like AAPS or pump, it will also be uploaded to Tidepool.

    Step 1 - Sign Up with Tidepool

    Visit tidepool.org
    Click "Personal Sign Up" or "Sign Up"


    Enter your email and create a password
    Choose "Personal Account" and click "Continue"


    Fill out the patient information and accept the terms of use
    Verify your email via the link sent to you

    Step 2 – Enter your credentials into xDrip+

    Go to and select Settings
    Go to Cloud Uploads


    Select Tidepool


    Turn on Sync to Tidepool


    Enter the email address you used during registration

    and password you used during registration for Tidepool

    Test the connection once done.


    Avoid using test servers (keep unselected).


    You may choose to upload under specific conditions to conserve battery life and minimize data usage costs.


    If you manually upload pump data, consider disabling xDrip+ treatment uploads to prevent duplicates.


    If upload fails, enable the new authentication protocol.



    Step 3 - Enter credentials into AAPS

    Open the “” Gear menu in the top left and select “Config Builder” (or find it among the tabs at the top).


    In the “Synchronization” section, check mark “Tidepool”.


    Click the gear icon next to “Tidepool”.
    and Enter your username and password created in Step 1 for Tidepool.


    Click “Test Tidepool Login”.


    If you see “Successfully logged into Tidepool”, you’re connected. If not, verify your credentials and email address.

    If You Don’t See the Success Message:

    Verify Your Email & Password.

    Ensure you’re using the exact email registered with Tidepool.

    Check for typos or extra spaces in your password.

    Now go to “Connection settings” and adjust according to your preferences.


    Thats all you need to do in AASP, If you need to setup a Nightscout Click Here . If you need any help on setting up Dexcom Clarity then see there Web site or call 0800 031 5763



    Upload Data

    Tidepool primarily syncs data automatically from supported devices (Dexcom, Omnipod, Medtronic, etc.), but you can also manually upload data from certain devices like blood glucose meters (BGMs) or older pumps.

    📥 Supported Devices for Manual Upload

    Blood Glucose Meters (BGMs) – OneTouch, Contour Next, Accu-Chek, etc.

    Insulin Pens (if using a smart pen with exportable data).

    Old Pump Data (if automatic sync isn’t supported).

    Note

    Dexcom, Omnipod 5/Dash, and Tandem pumps sync automatically—no manual upload needed.

    Steps to Manually Upload

    Download from Tidepool site by selecting Upload Data


    Download and install the Tidepool-Uploader-Setup-2.62.0 to your computer

    Log in


    Plug your device into your computer with a USB lead

        

    Select the upload button, related to your device type you are using.


    :pushpin: For more support on how to use your data once it is uploaded to Tidepool please visit: Visit tidepool.org
